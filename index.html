Welcome to Granite Alliance

Thank you for visiting Granite Alliance Insurance Company, a Medicare approved prescription drug plan. Granite Alliance is committed to innovation, efficiency, and excellent customer service. Granite Alliance works with employers and unions to provide a comprehensive Medicare Part D program that balances the need for managing pharmacy costs with quality care and high service.

Granite Alliance maintains a retail pharmacy network that includes over 65,000 pharmacies in all 50 states, the District of Columbia, and Puerto Rico. Granite Alliance members also have access to a mail order pharmacy that services all 50 states, the District of Columbia, and Puerto Rico.

We invite you to explore our website to find information and answers regarding your Medicare prescription benefits and access on our online resources.
