Representatives

There are two kinds of Representatives, the differences are explained below.

Appointment of Representative

Can be someone such as a relative, friend, advocate, doctor, or anyone else you want to act on your behalf to file a grievance, coverage determination, or appeal. This appointment can be completed by using the Appointment of Representative Form or an equivalent notice. For more information, please contact us.

A Representative to Release PHI

Can be someone such as a relative, friend, advocate, or anyone else you want us to disclose your personal, protected health information (PHI) to. You can give this access by completing the PHI Release Form. For more information, please contact us.

Note: Representative to Release PHI may not file a grievance, coverage determination, or appeal on your behalf.

Other persons may already be authorized under State law to act for you. Please contact us for more information.
