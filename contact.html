Contact Us

Granite Alliance understands that there are times when members have questions or concerns about their prescription drug coverage. In order for us to resolve your concerns quickly, please call Granite Alliance directly.

Medicare beneficiaries, members, pharmacists and physicians may contact us by one of the methods listed below.
